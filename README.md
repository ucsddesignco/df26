# Design Frontiers 2026

Design Frontiers is Design Co's annual designathon—a two-day sprint where teams tackle real-world challenges with creative design solutions. Final projects are presented to a panel of industry professionals who offer feedback and select standout work. Check it out: [df26](https://df26.designatucsd.com/)
